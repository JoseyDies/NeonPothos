Learning about the Neon Pothos

The neon pothos is a bright and beautiful plant that grows well inside the house. It's scientific name is Epipremnum Aureum, and it belongs to the family of Araceae.





Reasons why neon pothos makes a good house plant:

  • It's a hardy plant, which makes it good for beginners
  • Can grow in low light conditions
  • Enjoys fluorescent lights
  • Needs minimal watering
  • Cuttings can live in water for months without soil
